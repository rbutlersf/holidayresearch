**Outbound:** SFO → Frankfurt (FRA) → Milan Malpensa (MXP) - July 11-12
- **Flight time:** ~14 hours total (10h 30m + layover + 1h 15m)

### Important Italy Driving Notes

**ZTL Zones (Restricted Traffic):**
- Historic Bologna center has ZTL (automatic cameras issue fines)
- Your villa is OUTSIDE ZTL
- Park outside city center, walk in OR get hotel permit

**Autostrada Tolls:**
- Pay at toll booths (cash or credit card)
- Budget €40-60 for week

**Parking:**
- White lines = free
- Blue lines = paid (€1-2/hour)
- Yellow lines = no parking
- Villa has free parking

## FINAL NOTES & TIPS

### Money-Saving Strategies Actually Used

1. **Italo Family train offer:** Saves $250+ on Italian day trips
2. **Swiss Travel Pass:** Saves $750+ on Jungfraujoch alone
3. **Vacation rentals with kitchens:** Saves $1,000+ on meals
4. **Points redemption for flights:** Saves $6,000-9,000
5. **Free activities 50% of time:** Maintains budget balance
6. **Advance booking discounts:** Uffizi cheaper after 4pm, Fisherman's Bastion free before 9 AM

### Family Management Tips

**For 9-Year-Old:**
- Plan rest breaks every 2-3 hours
- Use gelato/ice cream as motivation
- Playground time essential (Margherita Gardens Bologna, Margaret Island Budapest, Interlaken parks)
- Activity books for long trains
- Let them help with simple navigation tasks

**For 4 Teenagers:**
- Give autonomy where safe (evening wandering in groups)
- Split activities by interest (paragliding vs. museums)
- Involve in photography/social media documentation
- Budget for shopping/souvenirs
- Food experiences usually unite everyone

**For 2 Adults:**
- Rotate "point person" daily (one manages logistics, other manages kids)
- Build in couple moments (sunset Danube cruise, thermal baths)
- Don't try to see EVERYTHING - quality over quantity
- Siesta time = adult rest too

### Weather Contingencies

**Italy Heat (If 95°F+):**
- Start activities 7 AM, finish 11 AM
- Museums/indoor 11 AM-5 PM
- Resume evening exploration 6-9 PM
- Double water intake

**Interlaken Rain:**
- Jungfraujoch often ABOVE clouds (still worth going)
- Backup: Trümmelbach Falls, chocolate factory, museum visits
- Always have rain jackets

**General:**
- Check weather forecast 2 days out, adjust accordingly
- Have 1-2 flex days for weather-dependent activities
