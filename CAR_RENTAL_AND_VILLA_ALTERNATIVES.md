## PART 1: CAR RENTAL RECOMMENDATIONS

### Why You Need a Car for Bologna-Area Villa

**Essential for:**
- Airport pickup (Bologna Airport to villa: 15-30 min drive)
- Grocery shopping (stock villa kitchen)
- Flexibility to leave villa for Bologna train station (20-30 min)
- Optional: Direct drives to day-trip destinations (though trains are easier)
- Getting to restaurants, wineries, local experiences

**Can skip car if:**
- Villa offers airport shuttle service (check with Palazzo di Varignana)
- You're comfortable with taxis/Uber for all transfers ($25-40 per trip)
- Villa is extremely close to Bologna Centrale (<10 min walk)

**My Recommendation: RENT THE CAR** - Gives maximum flexibility, especially with 7 people and luggage.

---

### Vehicle Requirements for 7 People

**You need:**
- 7+ passenger capacity (ideally 8-9 seats for luggage space)
- Automatic transmission (CRITICAL - Italian roads are hilly, narrow, stressful)
- Luggage capacity: 7 large bags + 7 carry-ons/backpacks
- Air conditioning (essential for July)

**Best vehicle class:** 9-seater minivan (seats 7 adults + has cargo space)

**Specific models available:**
- Mercedes Vito Traveliner (9-seater, automatic)
- VW Caravelle (9-seater, automatic)
- Ford Galaxy (7-seater, more cramped but available)
- Fiat Scudo (9-seater, often manual only)
- Opel Vivaro (9-seater)

### Driving in Italy - Key Considerations

**ZTL Zones (Zona Traffico Limitato):**
- Historic city centers often have restricted traffic zones
- Automatic cameras issue fines (â‚¬100-200 each!)
- **Solution:** Park outside ZTL, walk into center OR get hotel ZTL permit
- **Bologna has ZTL** - Your villa is outside it, but be careful if driving into city center

**Autostrada (Highway) Tolls:**
- Bologna â†’ Milan: ~â‚¬15
- Bologna â†’ Florence: ~â‚¬10
- Total estimated tolls: â‚¬40-60 for week
- Pay cash or credit card at toll booths

**Parking:**
- White lines: Free parking
- Blue lines: Paid parking (â‚¬1-2/hour)
- Yellow lines: Reserved (no parking)
- Your villa will have parking (confirm!)

**Gas:**
- Self-service ~â‚¬1.70-1.90/liter
- Full tank: ~â‚¬80-100
- Budget â‚¬150-200 for week of driving

**Important:**
- Drive on RIGHT side
- Speed limits: 50km/h city, 90km/h rural, 130km/h highway
- No phone use while driving (â‚¬160 fine)
- Headlights required on highways even during day
